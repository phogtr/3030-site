FAQ

Q: What platforms can this game be played on?

A: This game is only supported on PC and Mac.

Q: How do I Control the Character?

A: Players can move the character left and right by pressing the left and right arrow keys or pressing the “A” and “D” keys. The character can also jump by pressing either the spacebar or the “W” key. In order to defend themselves through the levels, they are able to cast a series of spells as they unlock them by exploring the levels. The water spell is available when the game starts and can be casted by pressing the “J” key. The following spells can be unlocked and used with their button when the player finds the corresponding spell. Fire can be casted by pressing the “K” key, ice is casted using the “L” key, lightning is casted by pressing the “;” key, and the earth spell is casted using the “I” key.

Q: What is the Objective?

A: The player’s primary objective is to complete all five levels and defeat the final boss at the end.

Q: How Many Characters Do I Control?

A: Players control a single character who is a young mage who wields elemental magic.

Q: Does the Game Have Multiplayer Features?

A: The game is currently only single player and does not have multiplayer features.
