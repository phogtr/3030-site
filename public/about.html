About Us

Our team, JPMAN Gaming Studios, consists of 5 members and the game was developed using the Godot engine. We wanted to make a game that was similar to other platformers to make it familiar to players of classics like Mario or Megaman. However, we wanted to create our own gameplay elements to make it unique. This includes the story, stage locations, unlockable attacks/spells, and more. We also wanted to explore the basics of the Godot engine and see what we could do in terms of creating a platformer.
