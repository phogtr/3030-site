Game Info

The year is 3030. An unknown curse is placed on earth and destroys the peaceful lives of the many, including our protagonist Marlious. His home gets obliterated by the curse and so, Marlious decides to travel across lands to find the answer to this cause. He explores many cursed places such as: the ghost town; the burning forest; the poison land; the heavy-storming city; and along the way, he finds the mysterious gems scattered around that help him with his journey. Eventually, he finds the lead to the final hideout of the dark lord who caused all of those events and Marlious must defeat him to bring back peace to his hometown and restore the balance of the earth.
